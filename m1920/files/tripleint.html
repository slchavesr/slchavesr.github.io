Triple integrals in Cartesian coordinates

This applet helps to visualize simple solids \(E\) of type I: - \( a \leq x \leq b\) - \( g_1(x) \leq y \leq g_2(y) \) - \( h_1(x,y) \leq z \leq h_2(x,y) \) Enter the corresponding bounds in the boxes, and use the buttons to see different projections of the solid.
