In the last years, I have dedicated my career to the transformation of math education as a more inclusive, inviting and engaging subject.

Mathematical Interests

  • Bringing Active Learning into the classroom.
  • Incorporating technological tools in different stages of the learning process.
  • Recognizing student's background and diversity and helping overcome their challenges.
  • Course, class activities and assessment design.
  • Technology based effective feedback methods.


I am currently a Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of Rochester. I finish my appointment on June 2024.

If you are a past, current or prospective student you access some information in the students tab

Other Projects / Interests

As a trained algebraic topologist in the classical sense, my research interests include
  • Equivariant cohomology for finite group actions.
  • Polyhedral products and connections with transformation groups and combinatorics
  • Neural Networks and Reinforcement Learning.
  • Guitar Playing and Videogames.

Short CV

  • Visiting Assistant Professor. University of Rochester. 2024.
  • Teaching Fellow. University of Western Ontario. 2020.
  • Visitor Fellow. Fields Institute. Toronto. 2020.
  • Ph.D. Mathematics. University of Western Ontario. 2020.
